Axioms: Kolmogorov's Axioms,

Positivity: \[\forall~E\in\Omega~\exists~\mathbb{P}(E)\geq 0.\]

Normalization: \[\mathbb{P}(\Omega)=1.\]

Additivity: \[\mathbb{P}\left(\bigcup_{i = 0}^{\infty}(E_i)\right)=\sum_{i=1}^{\infty}(\mathbb{P}(E_i)).\]
